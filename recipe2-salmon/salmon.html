This easy to make recipe comes together in twenty minutes. A light, fluffy, golden brown baked pancake comes out of the oven to please your taste buds.

Ingredients (serves 2 people)

  • 1/2 cup - All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup - Milk of any kind
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon - Sugar
  • 1 pinch - Salt
  • 1 pinch - Baking powder (may not be needed...experiment)
  • 1 dash - Vanilla Extract (optional)

Preparation

  • Place cast iron pan or dutch oven in the oven
  • Pre-heat to 425F
  • Mix all ingredients in a bowl and put aside

Instructions

  • Once at temperature, oil or butter the pan and pour the batter in it
  • Place in the oven
  • Set a timer for 12-15minute
  • Remove when golden brown.
  • DO NOT open the oven door or it will collapse. Look through the window
